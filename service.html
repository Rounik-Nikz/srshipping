Our Services Area

S.R. Shipping is a leading company specializing in the meticulous handling of Seafarer’s Flag Documents. With a client base ranging from Ship Owners to Crew Manning Agencies and Consultants, we proudly represent our partners with professionalism and expertise.

Our seasoned team possesses extensive experience and undergoes regular training to stay abreast of the dynamic shipping industry landscape. Rest assured, they are dedicated to providing prompt and top-tier service, guiding you seamlessly through every step of the Seafarer’s Documentation procedure.

We offer comprehensive Flag State Documentations for various flags, including Palau, St. Kitts and Nevis, Bahamas, Marshall Islands, Cook Islands, Liberia, Honduras, and Panama, tailored to your specific requirements. Our Crewing and Flag Documentation Services are adaptable and encompass the entire spectrum of Modern Crewing for Ship Management and Ship Owners, ensuring flexibility and reliability in every aspect of your maritime operations.
